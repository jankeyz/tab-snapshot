# Tab Snapshot — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 3, 2026*

Tab Snapshot is a browser extension made by one independent developer. The
short version: **your data never leaves your computer.**

## What the extension stores

When you save a session (or when the optional Auto-Snapshot feature runs),
Tab Snapshot records the **titles and URLs of your open tabs**. This data is
stored only in your browser's local extension storage
(`chrome.storage.local`) on your own device.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** send any data to the developer or to any server.
- It does **not** collect analytics, telemetry, or crash reports.
- It does **not** use cookies, fingerprinting, or any form of tracking.
- It does **not** show ads.
- It does **not** sell or share anything with anyone — there is nothing to
  sell, because nothing is collected.

## Permissions explained

- **tabs** — needed to read the titles and URLs of your open tabs so a
  session can be saved.
- **storage** — needed to keep your saved sessions on your device.
- **favicon** — needed to show the small site icons next to saved tabs,
  using Chrome's built-in favicon cache.
- **alarms** — needed for the optional Auto-Snapshot feature, which
  periodically saves your open tabs in the background (locally, like
  everything else).

## Payments

Supporting the project is voluntary and happens on [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/janko765),

sees or handles any payment information.

## Data removal

Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes everything it stored. You can
also delete any individual session from the popup at any time.
